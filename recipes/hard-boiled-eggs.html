Hard-Boiled Eggs

Description

Hard-boiled eggs have been cooked in the shell until their yolks are no longer runny. They can be prepared in a batch and then saved for later, either as a snack or as an ingredient in another dish.

Base Recipe

Ingredients

  • Eggs
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

Stovetop

  1. Put eggs in a large pot
  2. Fill with enough cold water to cover eggs
  3. Turn heat to high
  4. Salt the water
  5. Wait until water is almost boiling
  6. Put on lid
  7. Turn off heat
  8. Set timer for 17 minutes
  9. Drain water
  10. Run eggs under cold water
  11. Set timer for 20 minutes to cool
