White Rice

Description

Rice gives a warm, filling bulk to a quick meal. You can throw all sorts of things that you have laying around on top of a bowl of rice to make a meal out of them.

Base Recipe

Ingredients

  • White rice - 1c
  • Water - 1c
  • Salt

Steps (pressure cooker)

  1. Rince rice in strainer
  2. Shake out excess water
  3. Put ingredients in pot
  4. Close and seal
  5. Set to rice mode
  6. Wait for timer to ring
  7. Set timer for 10 minutes
  8. Release pressure and open pot
  9. Fluff rice
